THE FINAL GATE

I mark the line where shadows creep, Where silence sighs and secrets sleep. Beyond me lies what none have told, A whispered chill, a grip ice-cold. Step through my frame, you won’t return— Or if you do, you'll never learn. I’m not a lock, yet guard I must. I feed on fear, on hope, on trust. What am I?
